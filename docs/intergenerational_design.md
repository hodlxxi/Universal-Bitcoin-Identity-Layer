# Intergenerational Design

## Purpose
Explain why HODLXXI is framed for multi-decade continuity instead of short-cycle optimization.

## Time as system primitive
HODLXXI treats time as part of security and legitimacy:
- reliable behavior is demonstrated across repeated cycles,
- commitments are validated under changing conditions,
- social memory compounds through continued interaction.

## 21-year framing
The 21-year motif expresses a long-horizon commitment posture. It is a design orientation, not a deterministic guarantee of outcomes.

## Intergenerational continuity goals
- preserve context across participant turnover
- support accountable succession of Sponsor relationships
- reduce short-term extraction incentives
- keep coordination durable under changing narratives

## Practical implication for domains
Trust Domains that maintain records of interactions, Sponsor lineage, and pruning decisions generally retain stronger long-term coordination capacity.
