Core verification command

Run the public verifier from a checkout of the repository:

BASE="https://hodlxxi.com" JOB_ID="{{ proof.job_id }}" bash scripts/verify_paid_receipt_evidence.sh

What this proves

  • a public paid job record exists for the listed job ID;
  • public verification endpoints expose receipt/evidence data for that job;
  • the verifier script can check the relationship between job ID, receipt/evidence fields, attestation/reputation/chain-health surfaces, and signed/canonical public data as implemented by the current verifier;
  • the evidence is inspectable without account login or private secrets.

What this does not prove

  • does not prove legal identity;
  • does not prove KYC;
  • does not prove custody of funds;
  • does not prove locked capital;
  • does not prove that all future jobs will succeed;
  • does not prove that every advertised integration is production-ready;
  • does not replace application-specific risk checks;
  • does not require trusting a private screenshot.
