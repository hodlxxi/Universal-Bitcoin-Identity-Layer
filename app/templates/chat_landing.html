FAQ

What chat users need to understand first

Is this a normal chat account?

No. The identity model is key-owned. You enter by proving control of a cryptographic key.

Do I need a real name?

No. The app is designed for pseudonymous coordination.

Is this an identity product?

No. This page is about the chat app. Identity is key-owned and handled by the HODLXXI runtime.

Why does /app send me to login?

The chat app is auth-gated. If you are not logged in, HODLXXI sends you to login first and then returns you to /app.

Can I do video calls?

Yes. The chat app can include direct video calls and small group calls for up to 4 users.

Is Bitcoin payment required?

No. Bitcoin and Lightning are optional context. Chat and coordination are identity-first.

Does HODLXXI custody funds?

No. This landing describes communication and coordination. It does not create custody, escrow, or money transmission.
